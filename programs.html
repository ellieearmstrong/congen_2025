Intro

The tables below summarize some of the most commonly used programs for genome assembly, read mapping, variant calling, and related analyses. This is not an exhaustive list, but should help you recognize names you’ll see in the workshop and in the literature.

If you feel any information is inaccurate or out of date, or if you want to recommend a program to add to the lists, you can always compare against the original ConGen pages or your own notes.
